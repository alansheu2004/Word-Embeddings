Keith Silverstein is an American voice actor primarily known for roles in anime and video games. His most known roles include Vector the Crocodile in the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise since 2010, Simon Belmont in Castlevania Judgment, Hawk Moth in Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir, Torbjörn in the Overwatch series, Saber in the Fire Emblem series, Hisoka in Hunter × Hunter, Robert E.O. Speedwagon in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, and Zhongli in Genshin Impact.
In Super Mario-related media, Silverstein reprises his role as Vector in the Mario & Sonic series starting with Mario & Sonic at the London 2012 Olympic Games, and his role as Simon in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. He also provides the voice of the narrator in WarioWare: Move It!

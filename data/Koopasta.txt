Koopasta, also known as a Koopasta Dish in Super Paper Mario, is an item in the Paper Mario series. Tayce T., Zess T., and Dyllis can make this dish. It is made by mixing a Turtley Leaf with Dried Pasta in Paper Mario and Fresh Pasta in both Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door and Super Paper Mario. When used, it recovers both seven HP and seven FP in the first two games, and halves the amount of damage taken for 30 seconds in Super Paper Mario. The selling price for Koopasta is 25 coins in all shops except for Niff T.'s shop. In the international versions, the selling price is 60 coins in all shops.

Call for Backup is the tenth captain command in the Minion Quest: The Search for Bowser mode of Mario & Luigi: Superstar Saga + Bowser's Minions and is a first officer command used by Larry in the Bowser's Jr.'s Journey mode of Mario & Luigi: Bowser's Inside Story + Bowser Jr.'s Journey. In the former game, it costs four CP to use. In the latter game, it costs 5 CP and is unlocked when Larry reaches Level 29. When used, the captain will call upon a reserve member of their squad to back them up. Captain Shy Guy, Sergeant Guy, Magikoopas, and Ludwig von Koopa (second battle) are the only ones that can use it.

World 2 (also titled the Desert World) is the second world of New Super Mario Bros. Wii. It takes place in a desert. There are eight levels in this world, two of them being castle levels that are guarded by Roy Koopa. It also has a total of ten exits. Various Toad Houses are also found around here, giving the player items such as Stars, Super Mushrooms, and Propeller Mushrooms. On the far area of the map, holes, made by Spinies appear in the sand to try to drop Mario and the gang in, making them play in the Enemy Course, a level where they have to collect all the Toad balloons without losing a life.

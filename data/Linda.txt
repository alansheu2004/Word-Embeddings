Linda is a character from Mario Tennis: Power Tour. She holds the fourth rank in the Senior Class Doubles at the Academy. She is a speedy-type character, and her doubles partner is Shawn. She admits to getting very nervous before tennis matches, despite her tough demeanor. After losing to the player and Clay or Ace as their partner, she blames Shawn for losing before putting some blame on herself, and she takes this loss as a fresh start as the player moves up the ranks. She gets rank 3 in Senior Class Doubles with Shawn after the player places in Varsity Class Doubles.

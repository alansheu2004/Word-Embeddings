The Pink Capsule Kart is a High-End kart in Mario Kart Tour. It was introduced in the May 2022 Peach vs. Bowser Tour and is the signature kart of Dr. Peach. It is a pink variant of the Capsule Kart, including the tires, and it also has a pink and red seat. Unlike the Capsule Kart, this kart increases the duration of Jump Boost speed boosts and the number of points earned for performing a Jump Boost.

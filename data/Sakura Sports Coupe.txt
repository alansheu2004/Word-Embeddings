The Sakura Sports Coupe is a High-End kart appearing in Mario Kart Tour. Introduced during the 2023 Ninja Tour, it is a pink variant of the Sports Coupe with bright green accents and flower designs across the front and sides. Unlike the Sports Coupe and more akin to the Sports Coupe 2022, it uses the Wild Pink's tires. Also unlike the Sports Coupe, this kart increases the duration of Dash Panel boosts and the number of points received from using a Dash Panel.
